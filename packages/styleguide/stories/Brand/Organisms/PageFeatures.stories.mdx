## Featured Stats

Promote a single stat by setting the `featuresMedia` prop to `stat` and providing `statText`.

<Canvas>
  <Story
    name="Featured Stats"
    args={{
      cta: null,
      features: [
        {
          statText: '45 mil.',
          title: 'Media Inquiries',
          desc:
            'E-mail us at [press@codecademy.com](#) or [download our press kit](#).',
        },
        {
          statText: '170',
          title: 'Media Inquiries',
          desc:
            'E-mail us at [press@codecademy.com](#) or [download our press kit](#).',
        },
        {
          statText: '200+',
          title: 'Media Inquiries',
          desc:
            'E-mail us at [press@codecademy.com](#) or [download our press kit](#).',
        },
      ],
      featuresMedia: 'stat',
    }}
  >
    {(args) => <PageFeatures {...args} />}
  </Story>
</Canvas>
