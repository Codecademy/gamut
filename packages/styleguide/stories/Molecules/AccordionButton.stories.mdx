## Themes

### Blue

Blue accordion tops are only usable in dark contexts, such as the LE's
Help Menu.

<Canvas className="dark">
  <Story name="Blue" args={{ expanded: true, theme: 'blue' }}>
    {(args) => <AccordionButtonDeprecated {...args} />}
  </Story>
</Canvas>

### Plain

Plain AccordionButtonDeprecated tops are best applied in plain pages such as marketing pages
where we haven't been able to invest yet in a more solid theme.

  <Story name="Plain" args={{ expanded: true, theme: 'plain' }}>
    {(args) => <AccordionButtonDeprecated {...args} />}
  </Story>
</Canvas>
