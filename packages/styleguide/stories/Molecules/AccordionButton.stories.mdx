## Themes

### Blue

Blue accordion tops are only usable in dark contexts, such as the LE's
Help Menu.

<Canvas className="dark">
  <Story name="Blue" args={{ expanded: true, colorVariant: 'blue' }}>
    {(args) => <AccordionButton {...args} />}
  </Story>
</Canvas>

### Plain

Plain AccordionButton tops are best applied in plain pages such as marketing pages
where we haven't been able to invest yet in a more solid colorVariant.

<Canvas>
  <Story name="Plain" args={{ expanded: true, colorVariant: 'plain' }}>
    {(args) => <AccordionButton {...args} />}
  </Story>
</Canvas>

### Yellow

Yellow AccordionButton tops are used in the "Get a Hint" sections of the LE's
narrative pane.

<Canvas>
  <Story name="Yellow" args={{ expanded: true, colorVariant: 'yellow' }}>
    {(args) => <AccordionButton {...args} />}
  </Story>
</Canvas>
