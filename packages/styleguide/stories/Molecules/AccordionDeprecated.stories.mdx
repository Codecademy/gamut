## AccordionAreaDeprecated

If you'd like a "controlled" component equivalent, you can manually use the `AccordionAreaDeprecated` and `AccordionButtonDeprecated` sub-components.
Most accordions should use an `AccordionButtonDeprecated`; other forms of buttons are acceptable for custom accordions.
