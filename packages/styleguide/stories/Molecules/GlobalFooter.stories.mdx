## Common Geo Usage

Most geographies of usesrs, including `'US'`, will see all links in the footer: including the _Shop_ link.

## Indian Geo Usage

Users with `userGeo` set to `"IN"` will not see the _Shop_ link.
This is to comply with country regulations around our nascent Indian office.
