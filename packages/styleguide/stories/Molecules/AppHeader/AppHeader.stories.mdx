Uses <LinkTo kind='Molecules/AppBar' story="Header">AppBar</LinkTo> to arrange items determined by <LinkTo kind="Molecules/GlobalHeader" story="GlobalHeader">GlobalHeader</LinkTo> to the left or right side of the navigation bar. These items are passed in as objects of type AppHeaderItem, and map to <LinkTo kind="Molecules/AppHeaderLogo" story="AppHeaderLogo">AppHeaderLogo</LinkTo>, <LinkTo kind="Molecules/AppHeaderLink" story="AppHeaderLink">AppHeaderLink</LinkTo>, <LinkTo kind="Molecules/AppHeaderDropdown" story="AppHeaderDropdown">AppHeaderDropdown</LinkTo>, <LinkTo kind="Atoms/Button" story="TextButton">TextButton</LinkTo>, <LinkTo kind="Atoms/Button" story="FillButton">FillButton</LinkTo>, or a custom component passed in by the consumer.

AppHeader is not intended to be used directly by the consumer, but rather by <LinkTo kind="Molecules/GlobalHeader" story="GlobalHeader">GlobalHeader</LinkTo> which determines the composition of each version of Codecademy's global navigation header and passes the corresponding items to this component.
