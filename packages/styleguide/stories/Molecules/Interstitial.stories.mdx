## With Buttons

If the `buttons` props is provided, the components will render underneath the title and children.

<Canvas>
  <Story
    name="Single Button"

      buttons: [

        </ButtonDeprecated>,
      ],
      title: 'A Single Button!',

  >
    {(args) => <Interstitial {...args} />}
  </Story>

## Decorations

Decorations are added on top of the heading and are narrated as part of
it. Put emojis or promotional images here to really emphasize them.
