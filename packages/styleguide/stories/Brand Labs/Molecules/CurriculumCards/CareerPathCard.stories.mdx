## CareerPathCard with Enrollment Status

Unlike the old <LinkTo kind="brand-labs-molecules-curriculumcard--curriculum-card#career-path-description-variant">
CurriculumCard</LinkTo> designs, we show the same image for unenrolled, in progress, and completed full size career path cards. We achieve this by stripping "-inProgress" and "-completed" from the imageUrl.
