# AccordionButton

<StoryStatus status="volatile" />

The standard "top" button for an `Accordion`.
Comes in a few standard size and color combinations.

<Preview>
  <Story name="AccordionButton">
    <AccordionButton
      expanded={boolean('Expanded')}
      size={select('Size', ['normal', 'large'], 'normal')}
      theme={select('Theme', ['blue', 'plain', 'yellow'], 'plain')}
    >
      Click me!
    </AccordionButton>
  </Story>
</Preview>

<Props of={AccordionButton} />

## Themes

### Blue

Blue accordion tops are only usable in dark contexts, such as the LE's
Help Menu.

<Preview className="dark">
  <Story name="Blue">
    <AccordionButton expanded theme="blue">
      Wow, hidden treasure!
    </AccordionButton>
  </Story>
</Preview>

### Plain

Plain AccordionButton tops are best applied in plain pages such as marketing pages
where we haven't been able to invest yet in a more solid theme.

<Preview>
  <Story name="Plain">
    <AccordionButton expanded theme="plain">
      Wow, hidden treasure!
    </AccordionButton>
  </Story>
</Preview>

### Yellow

Yellow AccordionButton tops are used in the "Get a Hint" sections of the LE's
narrative pane.

<Preview>
  <Story name="Yellow">
    <AccordionButton expanded theme="yellow">
      Wow, hidden treasure!
    </AccordionButton>
  </Story>
</Preview>
