## Usage

Use breadcrumbs to help users understand where they are in a site's hierarchy and to navigate back to previous pages.

### Best practices:

- It's recommended to use breadcrumbs on pages with a hierarchy of multiple levels.

## Variants

### Default

Breadcrumbs can be solely used for navigation.
