## Usage

Use RadialProgress to display progress as a percentage of a whole.

## Children

Optionally, children may be provided to be displayed in the center of the circle.
This is useful for conveying a textual description of progress, such as a percentage of X/Y ratio.

<Canvas of={RadialProgressStories.Children} />

## Animating

Since `value` can be a `number[]`, given an initial value, end value, and duration, these will animate
smoothly upon being rendered.

Example: `<RadialProgress value={[0, 100]} duration={5000} {...args} />`

Check out the video example below.
