## Usage

This animation uses [Framer Motion](https://motion.dev/) to animate the expansion and collapse of a component. The initial and exit state is `"collapsed"` and the animation is `"expanded"`.

Wrap the `ExpandInCollapseOut` component with an `AnimatePresence` and use a state to control whether or not the children of the ExpandInCollapseOut component are rendered.
